Lokpriya Gopinath Bordoloi International Airport

GuwahatiASIndia

Lokpriya Gopinath Bordoloi International is named after the freedom fighter and former first Chief Minister of its home state, Assam. Its airport code comes from a former spelling of Guwahati: Gauhati.
