Don Mueang International Airport

BangkokThailand

Originally Bangkok International, Don Mueang Airport was renamed when Suvarnabhumi Airport opened in 2006. It gave up its BKK code and received a new one derived from Don Mueang and carrying over the ‘K’ from the old code.
