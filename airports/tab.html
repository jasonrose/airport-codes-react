A.N.R. Robinson International Airport

ScarboroughTrinidad and Tobago

Renamed in 2011, A.N.R. Robinson International honors Arthur Napoleon Raymond Robinson, the third Prime Minister of Trinidad and Tobago. Its three-letter code rearranges letters from its home on the island of Tobago.
