Durham Tees Valley Airport

DarlingtonUK

Commonly still called Teesside International, Durham Tees Valley started as RAF Middleton St. George in 1941. Some say its code comes from Middleton Military Establishment, but the airport itself has said the second ‘M’ is a filler letter.
