Bandar Udara Pattimura

AmbonIndonesia

Pattimura Airport is named after Thomas Matulessy, also known as Kapitan Pattimura, Indonesian soldier and national hero. Its airport code comes from the city of Ambon plus a ‘Q’, often used as a filler letter.
