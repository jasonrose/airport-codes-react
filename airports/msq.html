Minsk National Airport

MinskBelarus

Minsk National, formerly known as Minsk-2 (yes, there is a Minsk-1), is the primary airport in Belarus. Its code comes from the city of Minsk. The ‘Q’ works phonetically, but is used as a filler letter.
