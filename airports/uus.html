Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk Airport

Yuzhno-SakhalinskRussia

Started in 1945 as a military airfield, Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk Airport serves the city of Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk. Its code is completed by a second letter ‘U’, used as a filler letter.
