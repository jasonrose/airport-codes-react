Sir Gaëtan Duval Airport

Plaine CorailMauritius

Inaugurated as Plaine Corail Airport in 1972, it was renamed in 2007 in honor of Sir Charles Gaëtan Duval for his economic contributions. The only daily flights are between the islands of Mauritius and Rodrigues.
