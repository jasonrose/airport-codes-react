F. D. Roosevelt Airport

Sint EustatiusNetherlands

Originally Golden Rock Airport, F. D. Roosevelt Airport was renamed in 1946. It serves tourists to the dormant volcano “The Quill” and gets its code from Sint Eustatius, completed with an ‘X’, often used as a filler letter.
