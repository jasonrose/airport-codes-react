Aeroporto de São José dos Campos-Professor Urbano Ernesto Stumpf

São José dos CamposSPBrazil

Named after Urbano Ernesto Stumpf, aviator and innovator, São José dos Campos Airport gets its airport code from the city of São José dos Campos. The ‘K’ is a phonetic replacement for the ‘C’ in Campos.
