Princess Juliana International Airport

Simpson BaySaint MartinNetherlands

Many airports use the letter ‘X’ to fill in when the desired letters are already taken. Princess Juliana International was named after the crowned princess (at the time) of the Netherlands. Its airport code stands for Saint Martin, plus that helpful ‘X’.
