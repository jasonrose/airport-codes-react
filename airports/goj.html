Strigino Nizhny Novgorod International Airport

Nizhny NovgorodRussia

Colloquially called Nizhny Airport, Strigino International gets its code from Nizhny Novgorod’s former name of Gorky. The ‘J’ isn’t as clear and is likely a filler letter.
