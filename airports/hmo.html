Aeropuerto Internacional General Ignacio Pesqueira García

HermosilloSOMexico

Often called just Hermosillo Airport, General Ignacio Pesqueira García International is named after Ignacio Pesqueira, 19th century Mexican army general. Its airport code honors its home in the city of Hermosillo.
